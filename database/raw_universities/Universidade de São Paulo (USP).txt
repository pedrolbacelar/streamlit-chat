Established in 1934, the Universidade de São Paulo (USP) stands as one of Latin America's largest and most prestigious universities. Situated in the vibrant metropolis of São Paulo, USP embodies a rich cultural heritage and a commitment to academic excellence and social responsibility.

USP offers a diverse range of courses across various disciplines, including humanities, social sciences, engineering, and health sciences. With a focus on interdisciplinary collaboration and experiential learning, the university prepares students to tackle complex challenges and contribute to the advancement of society.

At USP, students engage in a vibrant academic community characterized by intellectual curiosity, cultural diversity, and a spirit of social engagement. The university's commitment to research and innovation is reflected in its state-of-the-art laboratories, research centers, and partnerships with leading institutions worldwide.

Beyond academics, USP offers students a rich array of extracurricular activities, cultural events, and community service opportunities. From student-run organizations to volunteer programs, students at USP are encouraged to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and make a positive impact on society.
